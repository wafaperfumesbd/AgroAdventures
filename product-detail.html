About This Product

Ilish Shutki is prepared using a traditional sun-drying method passed down through generations of fishing families around Chalan Beel. Fresh Ilish is cleaned, lightly salted, and laid out across bamboo racks for up to three weeks — turned daily to dry evenly without artificial heat or chemical preservatives.
