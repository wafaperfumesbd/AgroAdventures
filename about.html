OUR STORY

Rooted in Bangladesh's Largest Wetland

Chalan Beel Agro Ventures exists because of a simple belief: the best agricultural products come from people who know the land.

HOW WE STARTED

Why Chalan Beel

Chalan Beel Agro Ventures began with a straightforward frustration: products sold as "authentic Bangladeshi shutki" rarely came with any real account of where they were from.

We started working directly with fishing and farming families around Chalan Beel — buying at fair prices without layers of middlemen reshaping the price along the way.

Our Mission & Values
